Geometry series

2023

Installation

h160 × w300 × d150 cm

The Geometry series examines the tension between fragility and stability, reflecting the delicate balance of life’s embryonic stage. It represents a static form embodying time, change, and the coexistence of transformation and destruction. The precarious arrangement of objects within its organic structure highlights vulnerability yet paradoxically conveys stability, appearing on the verge of collapse while holding intact. Material distribution in the sculptures was determined using topology optimization, a computational technique that optimizes material use within a design space. This process creates chimera-like forms supporting disjointed, functionally unrelated objects without practical purpose, symbolizing the pursuit of unattainable goals. By blending algorithmic growth associated with "nature" and human elements tied to the body, the series challenges the divide between the natural and the human-made.
